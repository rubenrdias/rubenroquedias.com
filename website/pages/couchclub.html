This project is still ongoing.

The idea for this application emerged after having experience one too many spoils regarding some movie or tv show while reading in group chats.

The aim is to provide a place for people discuss at will with their friends any movie or show that they are currently watching. These are the planned features

  • Create watchlists, to track a group of movies or shows to watch later
  • Collaborative watchlists, to enable a group viewing experience
  • Search for movies or shows
  • Create chatrooms to discuss a certain watchlist, movie or show
  • iPad support for a seamless experience in all devices
  • Dark theme support for devices running iOS 13 or up

Relevant Technologies

  • Core Data - store all application data
  • Firebase - managing user accounts, storing application data, enabling live chats
  • User Defaults - storing any user settings

Implemented Screens

These are the currently functional screens, even if not all functionality is available.

Create your Watchlists

Begin tracking your favorite movies or shows by first setting up a watchlist.

Track your Progress

Keep track of the movies or shows you are seeing.

You can add/remove items from the watchlist, as well as mark items as seen.

Find Movies and Shows

Search for your favorite movies and shows and quickly get to what you are looking for.

You can also change the layout mode to make discovery easier, to see the results in a list or grid.

Detailed Information

See more information about the movie or show you want to explore.

You can then add it to a watchlist, or start a chatroom to discuss with friends.
