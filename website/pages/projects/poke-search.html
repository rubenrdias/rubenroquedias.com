Explore Your Location

See which pokémon are around you, based on other users' reports.

If you want to check out a location before heading there, simply navigate to it and any pokémon in the area will show up.

Report A Sighting

To help other players find more pokémon, easily report a sighting and they will be able to know where to go and look for it.

Just select the pokémon you just found, confirm the sighting, and they'll show up for other players to see.

Quick Directions

If you're exploring a place you don't know, or are having trouble getting to a sighted pokémon, you can get directions to its location.

Simply tap on a pokémon's image, then on the directions symbol in the popup, and Apple Maps will show you directions from your location.
