Instagram Clone
  •  Home

This app is an Instagram clone, that I built by following along LBTA's course by Brian Voong. Not all functionalities of the full instagram app were implemented, but the essentials have all been developed. The main functionalities are:

  • User registration and/or login
  • Creating posts, by taking new photos or importing from gallery
  • Commenting on posts
  • User search, user profile and following users
  • Receiving notifications upon having a new follower

A repository link will not be provided, as this is a paid course with intellectual rights over its contents.

Relevant Technologies

  • Firebase Auth, for user creation and authentication
  • Firebase Database, for storing posts, comments and likes information
  • Firebase Storage, for storing user profile and post images
  • Firebase Messaging / Cloud Functions, for sending notifications for new followers
  • AVFoundation, for capturing new photos

Get Your Account

Easily sign up to the application using your email, and provide a username and photo to use in your profile.

If you're a registered user, simply login and get back into your account.

See What's Going On

Scroll through your Feed, where you'll find updates from people you follow, as well as photos you posted.

Give feedback on posts using the Like feature, to let others know you enjoy their content.

Upload New Photos

Easily share photos with your followers, which will show on their feeds.

Choose an image from your gallery or take a fresh new picture with your phone's camera.

Discover Other Users

Search for other users to find someone you might like to follow.

Quickly preview their profile and scroll through the photos they have posted.

Share Your Thoughts

Easily let other people know what you think of their posts with a comment, or simply use this space to chat with other users.
